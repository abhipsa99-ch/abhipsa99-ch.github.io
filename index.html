My Journey

Who I Am

Hello! I'm Abhipsa Chakraborty, a Renewable Energy Engineer specializing in Wind Energy and a Biotechnology graduate. I am passionate about renewable energy, sustainability, research, and engineering solutions that create a positive environmental impact.

School Life

My interest in science began during my school years, where I developed curiosity about biology, mathematics, and problem-solving. These experiences inspired me to pursue engineering and scientific research.

Bachelor's in Biotechnology

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Biotechnology, gaining practical laboratory experience in microbiology, molecular biology, analytical techniques, and research. During this period I also completed an internship at IIT Kharagpur on biodiesel production from Jatropha plants.

Renewable Energy

To contribute to the global energy transition, I pursued the European Master in Renewable Energy, specializing in Wind Energy at the National Technical University of Athens. My studies focused on wind turbine aerodynamics, wind resource assessment, and renewable energy systems.

Looking Ahead

My goal is to contribute to innovative renewable energy projects while continuously learning, collaborating internationally, and supporting the development of sustainable energy solutions.
